# Valuation Campaign Playbook

## Purpose
For swing or long-term investments. Focuses on fundamental factors, PE ratio compression, and mean reversion.

## Triggers
- Stock trading near 52-week lows but with strong earnings.
- Sector rotation.
- Undervalued relative to peers.

## Rules
- Scale into positions over time (tranches).
- Stop loss is based on structural changes in the company, not tight price movements.

## Actionable Checks
When the user asks about an investment, use MCP tools to check fundamentals and present a "Valuation Campaign" report.
